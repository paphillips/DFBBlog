PSoC Microcontrollers With a Digital Filter Block Component

Kits

CY8CKIT-059

By far the easiest and lowest cost way to get started with DFB programming is to get a CY8CKIT-059. It includes a USB connection to power the board, integrated Kitprog programmer/debugger, and is a narrow board that fits great in a breadboard. Just solder the pin headers on, plug it in, and get started in PSoC Creator.

Buy a CY8CKIT-059 Now

CY8CKIT-050

If you want to get a larger prototyping board including an LCD display, the CY8CKIT-050 is an option. It has been designed for analog performance with close to 20 ENOB (Effective number of bits), and also supports several expansion boards.

Buy a CY8CKIT-050 Now

CY8CKIT-001

This kit is significantly larger and includes PSoC 1, 3, 4, and 5 plugin modules to evaluate different chip families, and the main board includes integrated peripherals such as LCD, breadboard, and GPIO connectors. For DFB development it is not the best option unless you specifically want to work with other PSoC famililies that do not include the DFB.

Buy a CY8CKIT-001 Now

Microcontrollers

There are a variety of PSoC 5 microcontrollers available individually once you are ready to move to your design.
